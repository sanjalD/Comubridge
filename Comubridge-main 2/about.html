About us

Communication is such an easy aspect of our daily lives, isn't it? Not for everyone. It started when a simple conversation was not possible between us and a sweet little girl who was deaf. That incident kicked off the cogs in our minds and led us to create Comubridge.

Comubridge is an All Inclusive
i.e. 5×4 Communication Platform which caters to the People who face Communication Difficulties as well as to the People Without Communication Difficulties. 

            Our 5×4 criteria includes:
            a. Deaf
            b. Mute
            c. Blind 
            d. Mentally Challenged 
            e. People without Communication Disabilities

Comubridge is a Bridge between Traditional and Non-Traditional Communication, bringing forth Inclusivity For All.
